Legends of the Game

Football has seen many greats, but these four have defined generations with their skill, leadership, and records.

Lionel Messi

Argentina | FWD

The most decorated player in history. Known for his low center of gravity and playmaking skills.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Portugal | FWD

The all-time leading goalscorer in official matches, famous for his incredible physique and dedication.

Diego Maradona

Argentina | AM

A genius with the ball who single-handedly carried Argentina to the 1986 World Cup victory.

Zinedine Zidane

France | CAM

Known for his elegance and the "Roulette" move. A champion for both France and Real Madrid.
