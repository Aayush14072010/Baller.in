Evolution of the Beautiful Game

1863 - The Split from Rugby

The Football Association (FA) was formed in London at the Freemasons' Tavern. They established the first 12 rules of the game, famously banning the use of hands and "hacking" (kicking shins), which split the sport from Rugby Football.

1872 - First International Match

The first official international football match was played between Scotland and England in Glasgow. It ended in a 0-0 draw, but it paved the way for international competition.
